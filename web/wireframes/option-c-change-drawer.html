Option C: Change Drawer

A right-side drawer panel shows all three change layers stacked vertically with collapsible sections. The drawer is togglable from the toolbar.

Concept: Like a "Source Control" panel in VS Code. The drawer lives alongside the detail panel and shows a cascading view: your local edits at top, branch changes in middle, staging changes at bottom. Each section is collapsible. A flow diagram shows the direction: you → branch → staging → main.

Pros
  • Full picture always visible without popover
  • Flow arrows make the promotion path obvious
  • Collapsible sections let you focus on one layer
  • Familiar "source control" metaphor
  • Natural place for merge/promote actions later
Cons
  • Consumes significant horizontal space (320px)
  • Detail panel gets squeezed on smaller screens
  • Three panels + drawer = four columns on desktop
  • Overkill for users who just want quick edits
  • Needs toggle to hide/show — more UI to manage
