definitionThe Geological Sample Material Type vocabulary is a SKOS-based hierarchical classification system for geological specimens. The scheme provides comprehensive coverage of the earth sciences domain by accommodating both natural and processed materials, terrestrial and extraterrestrial sources, and the practical needs of collection management across diverse geological institutions.
descriptionThe Geological Sample Material Type vocabulary is a SKOS-based hierarchical classification system for geological specimens. The scheme provides comprehensive coverage of the earth sciences domain by accommodating both natural and processed materials, terrestrial and extraterrestrial sources, and the practical needs of collection management across diverse geological institutions.
scopeNoteThe Geologic Specimen Material Type vocabulary is intended to provide a top-level catagorization scheme for specimens that belong to earth sciences collections. Usage beyond the scope of earth sciences collections may require adjustments or broadening of specific definitions.
usageNoteIntended to be used with the material category terms (e.g. dwc:materialEntityType) for specimens belonging to earth sciences and asteromaterials collections.
historyNoteCompiled by the TDWG Mineralogy Extension Task Group for community use and to support the extension release by providing standardized values for Material Entity Type, which is central to the Extension and Compound Specimen Model.
